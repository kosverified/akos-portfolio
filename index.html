About Me

I'm Akos — a videographer, editor, and director currently based in London. Over the last season, I travelled through Canada, the U.S., Switzerland, and Austria, shooting elite-level snowboard championships and working alongside world-class athletes. I’ve created content for global brands across sport and lifestyle, producing everything from fast-paced reels and short-form edits to cinematic branded campaigns and behind-the-scenes documentary-style vlogs. I focus on capturing real energy and building visual stories that connect with audiences across platforms.
