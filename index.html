About Me

I’m Akos Guld — a videographer, director, and editor based in London with experience across the UK, Canada, the U.S., Switzerland, and Austria. I specialize in cinematic storytelling with a strong focus on action sports, performance content, and high-impact commercial edits.

My work blends creative direction with fast, precise execution — supporting elite athletes and brands with media that performs on social and commercial platforms alike.

In the 24/25 season, I travelled to every Snowboard World Cup stop and the World Championships — filming in real-time, high-stakes environments and delivering content at speed.

Whether I’m directing long-form YouTube series, cutting fast-turnaround vertical edits, or shooting on-location, I bring narrative focus, efficiency, and creative clarity to every project.

For full projects, reels, or collaboration inquiries, feel free to get in touch.
